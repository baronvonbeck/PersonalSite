Briefly About Me


Born and raised in Sacramento, CA. Graduated UC Davis with honors, class of 2017.

I grew up around various computers; my dad has been building them since the 70's, so having our kitchen counter completely filled with pet projects we were working on together was extremely common. It was in middle school that I first started learning about the software side, and I knew then that was what I was going to base my career on. I love the feeling I get as I'm solving a "puzzle" and what I'm working on comes together, seeing decisions that I planned earlier on play out (hopefully for the better), learning new things and new ways of doing old things, and just simply building something (and the sense of accomplishment at its completion) that someone might find interesting or useful. I'm certainly not the most gifted person artistically, but software engineering allows me to exercise my creativity all the same.
